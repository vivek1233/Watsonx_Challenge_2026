### Enhancement Backlog Process

During managed service, client-requested enhancements are handled via:

1. **Enhancement Request** raised by client via ITSM portal
2. **Triage** by Application Support Lead (priority, effort estimate)
3. **Approval** by Service Delivery Manager + Client Product Owner
4. **Scheduling** into monthly enhancement release window
5. **Development** by MS team (L2/L3 engineers)
6. **Testing** in staging; client sign-off
7. **Deployment** to production in agreed release window
